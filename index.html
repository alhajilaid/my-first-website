In this system, we give an overview of an interactive educational learning tool which we have developed for kids aged from five to eleven years old in Gallisian Unit School of Technology. By developing this software, we aim at promoting self learning and developing information and communication technology skills among the youngsters in Gallisian Unit School of Technology. The educational system tool developed is based mainly for kids as tool for learning, it covered at lower pre-school and upper pre-schools levels, in Gallisian Unit School of Technology respectively.On the other hand, it will complement the existing teaching and learning methods.
In general, children are enrolled in primary schools at the age of six. They are admitted in Standard I and from there, they move automatically to higher standards until they reach Standard IV, where they are categorized via a streaming process. Preparation for the national examination, which is the Certificate of Primary Education, starts as from Standard V. The pupils must study five compulsory subjects, namely English, R.M.E, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.These pupils are also given the opportunity to study French language.
